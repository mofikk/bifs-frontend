About Building Oversight and Workplace Solutions

Building Oversight and Workplace Solutions is a specialized facility services enterprise that delivers integrated and comprehensive services to nonprofit organizations and small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs) through proven methodology of HONESTY, INTEGRITY, LOYALTY, and TRUST.

Reliable. Affordable. Eco-Friendly

BOWS is a professional facility management and cleaning company committed to creating clean, safe, and efficient environments. We provide integrated solutions that go beyond routine janitorial services, helping businesses, institutions, and communities focus on what matters most. From commercial cleaning to specialized industrial services, we combine expertise, innovation, and eco-friendly practices to deliver measurable results. Over the years, we have become a trusted partner for organizations that value reliability, professionalism, and sustainability.


We are committed to providing reliable and affordable cleaning solutions. Our eco-friendly approach ensures your space is clean and healthy, while minimizing environmental impact.
